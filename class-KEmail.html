Class KEmail

KEmail is an Yii Application Component that provides direct smtp interaction

KEmail wraps http://www.phpclasses.org/package/14-PHP-Sends-e-mail-messages-via-SMTP-protocol.html to provide a simple way to send email's to specific smtp servers from within php only. Does not require php's mail() or any other library.
